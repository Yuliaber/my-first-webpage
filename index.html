The British Shorthair Cat

The British Shorthair is one of the most famous cat breeds in the world. The British Blue variety is especially popular because of its grey-blue fur and copper eyes. This breed is known for its classic appearance and long history in Britain.

Why people like British Shorthair cats

  • Calm and friendly personality
  • Strong and healthy body
  • Easy to take care of

The history of the British Shorthair goes back many centuries. It is believed that Roman soldiers brought these cats to Britain. They helped protect food from mice and adapted well to the local climate.

In the nineteenth century, the breed was improved by breeders. One important person was Harrison Weir, known as the father of the cat fancy. He helped create breed standards and showed British Shorthairs at early cat shows.
